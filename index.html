Featured Viking Sagas

The Last Kingdom

2004

Young Uhtred is captured by Danes and raised as one of them. Torn between his Saxon heritage and his Viking upbringing, he must choose where his loyalties lie.

The Pale Horseman

2005

Uhtred finds himself at odds with King Alfred and in the midst of the Viking invasion. As he fights for his place, he faces personal and political challenges.

Lords of the North

2006

Uhtred seeks revenge against Kjartan the Cruel, who holds his sister captive. He must navigate treachery and deceit to reclaim his family honor.

Sword Song

2007

Uhtred is tasked with defending Alfred's kingdom from Viking invaders. His loyalty is tested as he must decide between his Viking roots and his duty to Alfred.

The Burning Land

2009

As Alfred's health declines, Uhtred must navigate the shifting power dynamics and protect the kingdom from both internal and external threats.

Death of Kings

2011

With Alfred's death, Uhtred must defend Wessex from ambitious rivals and invading forces while ensuring the kingdom's survival in a time of great uncertainty.

The Pagan Lord

2013

Uhtred is once again caught in the conflict between Saxons and Danes. His quest for power and revenge leads to epic battles and personal sacrifices.

The Flame Bearer

2016

Uhtred of Bamburgh finally has the chance to reclaim his ancestral home. But his enemies are determined to stop him at any cost, leading to a dramatic and climactic battle.

War of the Wolf

2018

Uhtred faces new threats from both the north and south. As he contends with rival factions and battles for control, he must navigate treacherous political landscapes.

Sword of Kings

2019

As England is torn apart by internal strife, Uhtred must defend his position and navigate the treacherous waters of politics and war to protect those he loves.
